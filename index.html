The Internet Of Things Requires Power

The internet of things connects every day objects to the Internet. This connection creates new and exciting ways to interact with and understand the world around us. Super localized weather forecasts, infrastructure monitoring, crowd metrics, and industrial monitoring are only a few of the emerging IoT markets. Common to all IoT devices is the requirement for power, usually with batteries.

Batteries have problems

Battery technology has advanced slower than electronics technology. Batteries are not environmentally friendly, require replacement and are expensive.

There is a better way

There is available free energy all around us in radio frequency waves, heat and vibration. We developed Zombee Tag technology to eliminate the need for batteries on IoT devices by capturing this available energy and converting it to electricity. Zombee Tags have no batteries, require no maintenance and are more environmentally friendly that traditional battery powered IoT devices.
